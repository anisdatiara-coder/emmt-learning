📋 Pipe Schedule & HAZOP/HAZID

Last Updated: 2024 | Level: Advanced | Est. Reading Time: 60 minutes

1. Pengenalan Pipe Sizing & Schedule

Apa itu Pipe Schedule?

Pipe schedule adalah standar untuk menentukan dimensi pipa (diameter nominal, outside diameter, wall thickness) dan pressure rating berdasarkan material dan kondisi operasi. Setiap schedule memiliki kombinasi ukuran dan ketebalan dinding yang berbeda untuk menahan tekanan yang berbeda.

Fungsi Pipe Schedule:

  • Safety: Memastikan pipa dapat menahan operating pressure dan design pressure dengan factor of safety
  • Standardisasi: Menggunakan size standar untuk ease of procurement dan replacement
  • Cost Optimization: Memilih schedule yang tepat untuk balance antara cost dan safety
  • Performance: Menentukan pressure drop dan flow velocity di sistem
  • Compatibility: Memastikan fitting dan equipment compatible dengan pipe size

Standar Pipe Schedule Internasional:

  • ASME B36.10M: Welded and seamless wrought steel pipe (Metrik dan Imperial)
  • ASME B36.19M: Stainless steel pipe (Metrik dan Imperial)
  • ISO 1127: Steel tubes - Outside diameters and wall thicknesses (Metrik)
  • DIN 2391: Precision steel tubes (German standard)

Pipe Schedule Designations:

  • Sch 10, 20, 30, 40, 60, 80, 100, 120, 140, 160: Increasing wall thickness untuk pressure rating
  • STD (Standard): Equivalent to Sch 40 untuk carbon steel
  • XS (Extra Strong): Equivalent to Sch 80 untuk carbon steel
  • XXS (Double Extra Strong): Thickest wall untuk highest pressure

2. Design Pressure & Wall Thickness Calculation

Formula Desain Tekanan Pipa (Barlow's Formula):

Barlow's Formula (most commonly used):

P = (2 × S × t) / (D - t)

Dimana:

  • P = Design Pressure (psi atau MPa)
  • S = Allowable stress material (psi atau MPa)
  • t = Minimum wall thickness (inches atau mm)
  • D = Outside diameter pipa (inches atau mm)

Factor of Safety dalam Desain:

  • ASME B31.3 (Process Piping): Factor of safety 3.5 untuk seamless pipe, 4 untuk welded pipe
  • ASME B31.8 (Gas Distribution): Factor of safety 0.5 hingga 0.72 tergantung class location
  • Corrosion Allowance: Additional thickness untuk anticipated corrosion (typically 1/16" atau 1.6mm)

Wall Thickness Selection Process:

  1. Tentukan operating pressure dan design pressure (biasanya 1.1-1.5x operating pressure)
  2. Tentukan material pipa dan temperature rating
  3. Tentukan allowable stress (S) dari design code (ASME B31.3, API 579)
  4. Hitung required wall thickness menggunakan formula
  5. Add corrosion allowance (1/16" - 1/8" typical)
  6. Round up ke schedule standar terdekat
  7. Verifikasi dengan code requirement dan factor of safety

Contoh Perhitungan:

  • Nominal Diameter: 2 inch (NPS 2)
  • Operating Pressure: 150 psi
  • Material: Carbon Steel (ASTM A53, Grade B)
  • Temperature: 250°F

Penyelesaian:

  1. Design Pressure = 150 × 1.1 = 165 psi (dengan 10% margin)
  2. Allowable Stress S = 15,000 psi (dari ASME B31.3 table)
  3. Outer Diameter D = 2.375 inch (untuk NPS 2)
  4. Required t = (P × D) / (2S + P) = (165 × 2.375) / (2×15000 + 165) = 0.013 inch
  5. Add corrosion allowance: 0.013 + 0.062 (1/16") = 0.075 inch
  6. Select Schedule 40 dengan wall thickness 0.154 inch (memenuhi requirement dengan FS > 3.5)

4. HAZOP (Hazard and Operability) Study

Apa itu HAZOP?

HAZOP (Hazard and Operability Study) adalah systematic dan structured methodology untuk mengidentifikasi potensi hazard dan operability issues dalam process systems. HAZOP dilakukan oleh tim multi-disiplin yang menganalisis setiap section (node) dari process dengan menggunakan guide words untuk generate deviations dari intended design.

Sejarah & Standar HAZOP:

  • Dikembangkan: Di ICI (Imperial Chemical Industries) pada 1970s
  • Standar Internasional: IEC 61882 (Hazard and Operability Studies)
  • Industri: Widely adopted di petrochemical, pharmaceutical, mining, utility
  • Referensi: CCPS Guidelines for Hazard Evaluation Procedures, API RP 750

Tujuan HAZOP:

  • Identify Hazards: Mengidentifikasi semua potential hazards yang mungkin terjadi
  • Assess Risks: Mengevaluasi consequences dan likelihood dari setiap hazard
  • Define Safeguards: Mengidentifikasi existing safeguards dan effectiveness-nya
  • Recommend Actions: Propose improvement atau additional controls untuk risk mitigation
  • Document Findings: Create comprehensive record untuk future reference dan follow-up

HAZOP Methodology - Step by Step:

  1. Define Study Scope: Tentukan section atau sistem yang akan distudi
  2. Form Team: Kumpulkan expert dari berbagai disiplin (Process Engineer, Mechanical Engineer, Instrument Engineer, Operator)
  3. Identify Nodes: Bagi sistem menjadi sections/nodes untuk analisis detail
  4. Apply Guide Words: Gunakan guide words (No, More, Less, Reverse, Other) untuk generate deviations
  5. Analyze Consequences: Tentukan apa yang terjadi jika deviation ini terjadi
  6. Assess Safeguards: Identifikasi controls yang existing untuk mencegah atau mitigate
  7. Risk Rating: Rate risk berdasarkan probability × severity matrix
  8. Define Actions: Tentukan tindakan yang diperlukan untuk mengurangi risk
  9. Document & Follow-up: Record semua findings dan track action items hingga completion

5. HAZOP Guide Words & Potential Deviations

Guide Words adalah systematic keywords yang digunakan untuk generate deviations dari intended design intent. Kombinasi guide word + parameter menghasilkan potential deviation untuk dianalisis.

Primary HAZOP Guide Words:

Guide Word Meaning Example Deviation Parameter
NO / NONE Complete negation of intent No flow, No temperature control, No pressure relief Flow, Temperature, Pressure
MORE / INCREASED Quantitative increase More flow, Higher temperature, Higher pressure Flow, Temperature, Pressure, Level
LESS / DECREASED Quantitative decrease Less flow, Lower temperature, Lower pressure Flow, Temperature, Pressure, Level
REVERSE Opposite flow or action Reverse flow (backflow), Reverse rotation Flow direction, Rotation, Circulation
OTHER THAN / DIFFERENT Qualitative change Wrong material, Contamination, Wrong composition Material, Composition, Phase

Contoh HAZOP Analysis untuk Pump Discharge Line:

Node Parameter Guide Word Deviation Cause Consequence Safeguard Action
Pump Discharge Flow NO No flow through discharge Pump cavitation, Blocked discharge valve, Pump failure Loss of system pressure, Pump damage, Shutdown Pressure switch with alarm, Manual check Install flow indicator (FI)
MORE Higher flow rate than design Pump overspeed, Bypass valve stuck open, Control valve failure High velocity, Erosion/corrosion, Pipe rupture risk, Vibration Relief valve set at max, Design pipe for max flow Upgrade relief valve setting
LESS Lower flow rate than design Partial blockage in piping, Valve partially closed, Pump degradation Reduced production capacity, Insufficient cooling/heating Visual inspection, Flow meter Schedule periodic piping inspection
REVERSE Backflow dari discharge line Check valve failure, Reverse pressure from downstream Pump damage, Product contamination, Loss of system integrity Check valve on discharge Periodic check valve testing

6. HAZID (Hazard Identification) Study

Apa itu HAZID?

HAZID (Hazard Identification) adalah qualitative, brainstorming-based approach untuk mengidentifikasi broad categories dari potential hazards dalam sistem design tahap awal. HAZID dilakukan lebih awal dalam project lifecycle dibanding HAZOP untuk identify high-level risks sebelum detailed design.

Perbedaan HAZOP vs HAZID:

Aspek HAZOP HAZID
Timing Detailed design phase atau commissioning Concept atau early design phase
Methodology Systematic, guide-word based, structured Qualitative, brainstorming based, checklist approach
Detail Level Highly detailed, line-by-line analysis Broad, high-level overview
Documentation Comprehensive, detailed recommendations Summary of key hazards untuk further study
Duration Weeks to months Days to 1-2 weeks
Cost Higher, resource intensive Lower, quick assessment

HAZID Typical Hazard Categories:

  • Loss of Containment: Leakage atau rupture dari pipa, vessel, equipment
  • Overpressure: Tekanan berlebih yang bisa cause rupture
  • Under-pressure: Tekanan rendah yang bisa cause implosion atau malfunction
  • Thermal Events: Thermal runaway, fire hazard dari fluid panas
  • Toxic Release: Release dari toxic substance ke environment
  • Equipment Failure: Pump failure, valve failure, control system failure
  • Vibration & Noise: Excessive vibration dari rotating equipment
  • Corrosion & Erosion: Material degradation dari corrosive atau erosive fluid
  • Blockage: Pipe blockage dari deposits, crystals, atau foreign material

7. Risk Assessment & Matrix

Risk Definition:

Risk = Probability × Consequence (Severity)

Risk Rating Scale:

Rating Probability (Likelihood) Consequence (Severity) Description
1 (Low) Remote, unlikely to occur Minor injury atau minor equipment damage Acceptable risk, no action required
2 (Medium) Unlikely, but possible Serious injury atau significant damage Monitor, define improvements untuk reduce risk
3 (High) Possible, likely to occur sometime Major injury atau fatality, major damage Immediate action required untuk reduce risk

8. Tipe-Tipe Kegagalan Pipa Umum

Failure Mechanisms dalam Sistem Piping:

a. Stress Corrosion Cracking (SCC)

Penyebab: Kombinasi dari tensile stress dan corrosive environment

Material Susceptible: Stainless steel, Carbon steel dengan chloride

Prevention: Material selection, stress relief, corrosion inhibitor

b. Erosion-Corrosion

Penyebab: High velocity fluid dengan erosive particles atau corrosive fluid

Location: Elbow, reducer, valve seat (turbulent zones)

Prevention: Lower velocity limit (< 6-10 ft/sec), smoother bend radius

c. Corrosion / Uniform Attack

Penyebab: Chemical attack dari fluid terhadap pipe material

Material Selection: Sesuaikan material dengan media (SS untuk acid, carbon steel untuk basic)

Monitoring: Regular inspection, ultrasonic thickness testing

d. Fatigue Cracking

Penyebab: Cyclic stress dari vibration, thermal cycling, pressure cycling

Critical Points: Support locations, pipe bends, branch connections

Prevention: Proper support design, vibration dampening, allow thermal expansion

e. Water Hammer / Surge Pressure

Penyebab: Sudden closure dari valve, pump shutdown, rapid velocity change

Consequence: Extreme transient pressure spike bisa rupture pipa

Prevention: Slow valve closure, surge tank, pressure relief valve

f. Mechanical Damage

Penyebab: External impact, improper handling, construction damage

Prevention: Protective covers, careful handling, quality control during installation

9. Metode Inspeksi Pipa

Visual Inspection (VI):

  • Dilakukan secara visual untuk identify obvious defects, leaks, deformation
  • Frekuensi: Rutin (harian/weekly), atau sesuai monitoring plan
  • Limited: Tidak bisa detect internal defects atau sub-surface cracks

Ultrasonic Thickness Testing (UT):

  • Menggunakan ultrasonic wave untuk measure sisa wall thickness pipa
  • Dapat detect corrosion/thinning yang sudah terjadi
  • Non-destructive, dapat repeat di lokasi yang sama untuk trending
  • Frekuensi: Annual hingga every 5 years tergantung service

Radiography (X-ray / Gamma Ray):

  • Menggunakan radiation untuk penetrate pipa dan detect internal defects
  • Dapat identify cracks, porosity, lack of fusion di welds
  • Destructive (safety hazard), lebih mahal dan kompleks
  • Biasanya untuk inspection weld quality, tidak routine maintenance

Eddy Current Testing (ET):

  • Menggunakan electromagnetic induction untuk detect surface cracks dan defects
  • Tidak bisa detect subsurface defects
  • Cepat dan portable, cocok untuk scanning wide areas

11. Best Practices dalam Piping System Design & Operation

Design Practices:

  • Proper Schedule Selection: Gunakan schedule yang tepat untuk design pressure dengan adequate factor of safety
  • Material Compatibility: Sesuaikan material dengan fluid service (chemical compatibility)
  • Support Design: Provide adequate support untuk minimize vibration dan thermal stress
  • Pressure Relief: Install pressure relief valve yang properly sized dan set
  • Check Valves: Install pada pump discharge untuk prevent backflow
  • Isolation Valves: Install untuk facilitate maintenance tanpa shutting entire system

Operational Practices:

  • Regular Inspection: Visual inspection rutin untuk identify early signs of problems
  • UT Testing: Schedule periodic UT testing di high-risk areas (elbows, reducers, corrosive service)
  • Pressure Monitoring: Maintain pressure gauge readings untuk track system health
  • Temperature Control: Maintain temperature di design range untuk prevent thermal stress
  • Velocity Control: Monitor flow velocity untuk prevent erosion-corrosion
  • Record Keeping: Maintain detailed inspection records untuk trending dan compliance

Common Mistakes to Avoid:

  • ❌ Oversizing pipes untuk anticipated future capacity (waste cost, low velocity problems)
  • ❌ Undersizing pipes untuk save cost (high velocity, erosion, energy loss)
  • ❌ Ignoring corrosion allowance di aggressive service
  • ❌ Not considering thermal expansion dalam support design
  • ❌ Skipping HAZOP study untuk "simple" systems
  • ❌ Neglecting routine inspection hingga ada masalah
